Vehicle E-Complaint Management System

Vehicle E-complaints Management System will provide a GUI based platform to the public to upload the rules violated vehicle information with photos, view the vehicle information, fine details and also the complaint status. The system will validate and verify the information and sends the reports the to the traffic police department. The reports are verified by the Authorised officer and if necessary a complaint will be filed. The list of filed complaints collected by the traffic police department will be uploaded in the website.
